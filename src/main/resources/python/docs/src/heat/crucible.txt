Industrial Crucible
crossroads:heating_crucible
The <item>Industrial Crucible/$ is a crafting machine that converts items into liquids using heat. It only works above 1000°C.
It drains heat constantly while above 1000°C, but will work faster and drain heat more quickly at higher temperatures. The efficiency stays the same.
<item><link:essentials:heat/cable>Heat cables/$ and pipes can connect to any side other than the top. Items can be inserted into any side.
This machine is typically used for, among other things, producing lava and melting metal items into a liquid. It can also double ores by melting them into 2 ingots worth of molten metal, which can be cooled into ingots in a <item><link:essentials:heat/fcc>Fluid Cooling Chamber/$.
