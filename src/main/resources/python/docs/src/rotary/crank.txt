The <item>Hand Crank/$ is a way of manually generating a small amount of <thing>rotary energy/$ early game.
Hold down right-click with a crank on any gear to slowly add energy at a constant rate. Hold shift while doing this to add energy in the opposite direction.
The <item>hand crank/$ is meant for the <em>very/$ early game and for testing setups- if you're finding yourself relying on it, try something like a <item><link:essentials:rotary/wind>Wind Turbine/$ instead.
