The <item>Tesla Ray/$ is an electrical AOE weapon. In order to fire, it requires a <item><link:essentials:fluid/tesla>leyden jar/$ in the offhand as a power source.
When fired, it will use 1000FE per shot, and will chain between <em>unlimited targets/$ within 5 blocks of each other for 7 electric damage.
It has an attack cooldown like a melee weapon, and will do less damage and <em>won't chain attacks unless fully cooled-down/$.
